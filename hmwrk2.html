Peanut Butter Cups Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3-4 cups powdered sugar
  • 12 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, cream together peanut butter, softened butter, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth.
  3. Add powdered sugar, 1 cup at a time, until the mixture is thick and dough-like. You may need to adjust the amount of powdered sugar depending on the consistency of the mixture.
  4. Melt chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl, stirring every 30 seconds until fully melted.
  5. Place a spoonful of melted chocolate into each muffin liner, spreading it up the sides of the liner with a spoon or pastry brush.
  6. Scoop a tablespoon of the peanut butter mixture into each liner on top of the chocolate layer.
  7. Top each cup with another spoonful of melted chocolate, spreading it to cover the peanut butter layer.
  8. Chill the peanut butter cups in the fridge for at least 30 minutes or until the chocolate has hardened.
  9. Remove the paper liners and serve the peanut butter cups chilled.
